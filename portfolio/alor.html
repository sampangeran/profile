Volunteer Teaching at Alor, East Nusa Tenggara

  1. Home
  2. Teaching at Alor

Summary

Alor is a regency located in East Nusa Tenggara, Indonesia with population of 213.994 inhabitants (2021) with an area of 2.928,88 km2. Majority of Alor's residents are Christians (74.80%), followed by Muslim (25.14%), and the rest are Buddhist and Hinduism.

Together with STEMI Jakarta and Surabaya, I went to Alor to share the gospel to elementary, middle and high school Christian students in the Kalabahi and East Pantar sub-districts. I served approximately 700 students and 50 Christian teachers for 7 days. Most of them felt delighted and thankful, and once again, they were given firmness of faith through the gospel.
